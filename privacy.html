Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your financial data in our personal finance tracking application.

Last Updated: July 29, 2025

Introduction

EasyKharcha ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EasyKharcha personal finance application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our finance tracking application available on Google Play Store.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and only collect data essential for the app's functionality. We do not sell your data to third parties.

COMPLIES WITH PLAY STORE REQUIREMENTS, GDPR & CCPA

Information We Collect

We only collect data essential for the app's core functionality:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Your email address collected through Firebase Authentication

Financial Data

  • Income sources and amounts
  • Expense categories and transaction details
  • Budget limits and financial goals

Optional Information

  • Referral codes (if provided by you)

Technical Data

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage analytics via Firebase Analytics

We explicitly DO NOT collect: Bank credentials, government IDs, precise location data, contact lists, or any sensitive personal information beyond what's required for the app's functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized expense tracking and financial insights
  • To sync your financial data securely across your devices
  • To generate reports and visualizations of your spending habits
  • To improve app functionality through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback
  • To verify account ownership and prevent fraud

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We adhere to strict data sharing principles:

Limited Service Providers

  • Data is processed through Google Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, and Analytics) under Google's privacy terms
  • Firebase services help us securely store and manage your financial data

Legal Compliance

  • Information may be disclosed if required by law enforcement, court order, or legal proceedings

Strict No-Sharing Policy

  • Your data is never sold to third parties
  • Your data is never shared with advertisers or marketers
  • Your data is never used for purposes unrelated to the app's functionality

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your financial information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256 encryption)
  • Access Controls: Firestore security rules restrict database access to your account only
  • Infrastructure Security: Leveraging Google Firebase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • Authentication: Secure password handling through Firebase Authentication

User Responsibility: While we implement industry-standard security measures, you play a crucial role in protecting your information. Please safeguard your login credentials and ensure your device security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your financial data:

  • Delete Account: Permanently remove all your data via the "Delete Account" option in App Settings
  • Data Access: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Data Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Disable through your device settings
  • Withdraw Consent: Uninstall the App to halt data collection

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Until you delete your account via the in-app function
  • For inactive accounts: Data is automatically deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Prior to deletion of inactive accounts, we will notify you via email

International Data Transfers

Data is processed via Google's global Firebase infrastructure. Google adheres to international data protection standards through:

  • GDPR compliance for European users
  • CCPA compliance for California residents
  • Google's Data Processing Addendum

Children's Privacy

EasyKharcha is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

Policy Updates

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision
  • Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance

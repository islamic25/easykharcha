Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Registration Data: Your email address and password during account creation
  • Financial Data: Income, expenses, withdrawal requests, and transaction history
  • User Activity: Daily task completions, rewards earned, and app interactions

Technical Information

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID), IP address, device model, OS version
  • Usage Data: Timestamps, feature usage, session duration, and crash reports

Ad-Related Data

  • Ad Interactions: Views, clicks, and interactions with AdMob banner and rewarded video ads
  • Advertising ID: Used for personalized advertising (based on your device settings)

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • Manage your account and authenticate users via Firebase Authentication
  • Track your income, expenses, and financial transactions
  • Process rewards from daily tasks and withdrawal requests
  • Provide personalized app experience and features

Analytics & Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues and app crashes
  • Develop new features and services based on user behavior

Advertising

  • Display AdMob banner and rewarded video ads
  • Measure ad performance and relevance using your Advertising ID
  • Generate revenue to support app development and maintenance

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that collect information:

Firebase Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Securely stores your email and password
  • Firestore Database: Hosts financial data, user activity, and withdrawal requests
  • Firebase Storage: Stores app-related files (profile avatars, documents)
  • Data Location: Firebase servers are global; your data may be processed outside your country

Google AdMob

  • Displays advertisements within the app
  • Collects your Advertising ID for personalized ads (based on device settings)
  • Provides ad fraud prevention and analytics services
  • Opt-Out: You can disable ad personalization via your device's Google Ads Settings

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Limited sharing occurs only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Google for Firebase and AdMob services (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law (e.g., court orders, legal investigations)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Note: Aggregate or anonymized data that cannot identify you may be shared with partners for analytics and research purposes.

Data Retention & Security

Retention Period

  • We retain your personal data as long as your account is active
  • After account deletion, data is removed within 30 days
  • Backup data may persist for up to 90 days post-deletion
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes

Security Measures

  • Firebase encrypts data both in transit (using SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Authentication safeguards to prevent unauthorized access
  • You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & Correction: View and edit your data directly in the App
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion via support email (processed within 30 days)
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Advertising Preferences: Opt-out of personalized ads via device settings
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

Age Restrictions

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors
  • If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal data should contact us
